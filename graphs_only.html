Safety Factor
Safety factor = anchor rating ÷ worst per‑anchor demand (dyno, k = 3). It tells you how many times stronger the anchor is than what it actually needs to handle. Above 2.5× is comfortable. 1.5× to 2.5× needs caution. Below 1.5× is not safe.
⚠️ Per‑anchor values assume perfect load sharing; in practice one strap may carry most of the load.
📄 Impact catches (Section 7 of the PDF) can reach 18–37 kN total, but are unlikely at typical angles.
